This tightly-held and much loved residence is being offered to the market for the first time in 30 years and only the second time since built. Whisper-quiet aside from the calming sound of birdlife, this highly convenient location is just a short walk to shops, cafes, the train station, Beecroft Primary and Cheltenham Girls High.
